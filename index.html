Profesional Boxing Summary

62 Wins, 8 Losses, 2 Draws

1997 - Won OBPF flyweight title against Chokchai Chockvivat

1998 - Won WBC flyweight title against Chatchai Sasakul

1999 - Won vacant WBC International super bantamweight title against Reynante Jamili

2001 - Won IBF super bantamweight title against Lehlohonolo Ledwaba

2003 - Won The Ring featherweight title against Marco Antonio Barrera

2005 - Lost IBA and vacant WBC International super featherweight titles Érik Morales

2005 - Won vacant WBC International super featherweight title against Héctor Velázquez

2008 - Won WBC and vacant The Ring super featherweight titles against Juan Manuel Márquez

2008 - Won WBC lightweight titlea gainst David Díaz

2009 - Won IBO and The Ring light welterweight titles against Ricky Hatton

2009 - Won WBO welterweight title against Miguel Cotto

2010 - Won vacant WBC super welterweight title against Antonio Margarito

2012 - Lost WBO welterweight title against Timothy Bradley

2013 - Won vacant WBO International welterweight title against Brandon Rios

2014 - Won WBO welterweight title against Timothy Bradley

2015 - Lost WBO, WBA (Unified), WBC, and The Ring welterweight titles against Floyd Mayweather Jr.

2016 - Won vacant WBO International welterweight title against Timothy Bradley

2016 - Won WBO welterweight title against Jessie Vargas

2017 - Lost WBO welterweight title against Jeff Horn

2018 - Won WBA (Regular) welterweight title against Lucas Matthysse

2019 - Won WBA (Super) welterweight title against Keith Thurman

2021 - Lost WBA (Super) welterweight title against Yordenis Ugás
